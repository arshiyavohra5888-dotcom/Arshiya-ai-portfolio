# AURELYN – AI-Assisted Brand Identity & Product Packaging System

## Overview

AURELYN is a conceptual premium natural foods brand created using AI-assisted design workflows.

The objective was to develop a complete visual identity system, including brand naming, logo creation, product packaging, product-specific labels, and marketing-ready product mockups.

The project demonstrates how generative AI can be used to accelerate brand development while maintaining a cohesive and professional visual identity.

## Brand Identity

### Brand Name: AURELYN

The name was developed to communicate:

* Natural Ingredients
* Purity
* Premium Quality
* Sustainability
* Modern Wellness

The goal was to create a brand that feels trustworthy, elegant, and connected to nature.

---

### Logo Design

The logo features two minimalist leaves.

The design symbolizes:

* Nature
* Growth
* Sustainability
* Freshness
* Organic Sourcing

A refined serif wordmark was paired with the leaf icon to create a premium and timeless identity.

**Tagline**

"Crafted From Nature."

## Packaging System
