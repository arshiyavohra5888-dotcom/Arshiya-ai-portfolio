# SAIRAJ – AI-Assisted Restaurant Brand Development

## Overview

SAIRAJ is a concept restaurant brand inspired by traditional Indian cooking, heritage dining experiences, and authentic flavors prepared in clay pots.

The project involved creating a complete visual identity system including brand naming, logo development, food cart design, menu creation, promotional materials, and marketing assets using AI-assisted creative workflows.

The goal was to create a brand that feels warm, authentic, premium, and deeply rooted in Indian culinary traditions.

## Brand Concept

### Brand Name: SAIRAJ

SAIRAJ was developed as a heritage-inspired food brand focused on authentic Indian cuisine.

The concept celebrates traditional recipes, slow cooking methods, and the cultural experience of dining.

---

## Brand Identity

### Logo Design

The logo features an ornamental clay pot with steam rising from it.

The symbol represents:

* Traditional Indian Cooking
* Authentic Recipes
* Warm Hospitality
* Heritage Food Culture
* Homemade Flavors

The circular ornamental border reinforces the premium and cultural character of the brand.

---

## Tagline

"Where the tradition simmers in a clay pot."

The tagline highlights the restaurant's focus on traditional cooking methods and authentic flavors.

---

## Visual Design System

The brand uses:

* Deep maroon tones
* Warm beige backgrounds
* Traditional decorative motifs
* Elegant typography
* Heritage-inspired illustrations

The visual identity was designed to evoke nostalgia and authenticity while maintaining a premium appearance.

---

## Restaurant Food Cart Concept

A complete food cart visualization was created to showcase how the brand could appear in a real-world environment.

Key features include:

* Premium wooden finish
* Integrated menu display
* Traditional Indian branding
* Modern food service layout
* Warm ambient lighting

The concept combines traditional aesthetics with contemporary food service design.
